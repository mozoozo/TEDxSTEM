Our Mission

Educate

The ability to unleash all of our mental capabilities to serve us in solving our problems or creating something useful and unique, known as creativity which many of the students' community have a few knowledge about and how to use it properly is our goal.

heart_plus

Inspire

Under the slogan of “The voice of the voiceless”, TEDx Youth Red Sea STEM encourages either dreamers, innovators, or thinkers to share their ideas and beliefs on a stage in front of more than 300 attendees and start to change their ambitions and lives

Our Vision

emoji_objects

Creative Impact

TEDx Red Sea STEM aims to organize an event that will gather various highly experienced speakers to give talks which will be in general including the connotation of creativity and illustration. As a non-profit organization, our agenda is to make great ideas accessible and spark conversation. A dynamic open community that continually embraces new ideas that impact the social, cultural, economic, environmental and educational fabric of Red Sea governorate and the world
